2. Description of Service

UNAWAIN is an on-device document intelligence application that:

  • Scans and extracts text from documents using your device's camera
  • Records and transcribes audio using on-device speech recognition
  • Imports and analyzes PDF, image, and document files
  • Generates AI-powered summaries, notes, checklists, and data tables
  • Translates content into Filipino, Cebuano, Ilocano, Pangasinan, Waray, and English
  • Identifies document types, named entities, key figures, and sensitive information

All processing occurs entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.
